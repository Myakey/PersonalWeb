Whale Fun Fact!

Some Whales are very social!

Some whales, like Orcas are highly social and often travel in groups in which the family focus on the mother.

They are long living!

Scientist estimates that whales can live up to more than 200 years old.This means they can be 3x as old as a human!

They breathe on lungs!

Unlike humans that have nostrils, which are seperate, whale's blowhole is a single opening that connnect directly to the lungs.
